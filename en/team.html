Team Introduction

CCM - HBQ Team

China Conservatory of Music - Han Baoqiang Team

HBQ Team pays attention to the actual needs of modern society for MAT talents, and strives to cultivate academic and applied talents with innovative awareness of MAT literacy.

FD-LAMT

Fudan University - Lab of Audio and Music Technology

FD-LAMT is committed to scientific research and development in the fields of Music AI, Computer Audition, Speech Information Processing, and Audio Information Security.

NEXT

Zhejiang University - NEXT Lab

NEXT Lab is committed to the research of improving human well-being, mainly focusing on Artificial Intelligence, Big Data, Music Emotion Computing and Information and Interaction Design.
