Obtaining complete files:

All data in this platform are open to everyone for partially free. Please follow the instructions below and submit the "Application Form" through e-mail to obtain it. Due to the need for manual evaluation, our reply may be slightly delayed.

1. Download "Application Form"

  Application Form.pdf

2. Please read the statement below the application form carefully. If you agree with all the contents, please check "Agree"; If you do not agree with the content of the undertaking, we will not be able to provide you with the complete data.

3. After completing all the required information in the application form, please sign or insert your electronic signature in the "Applicant" box below, then save it as a PDF and email the application form to the following email address:

  ccmusic.database@hotmail.com

4. After receiving your application form, we will evaluate whether we can provide you with the complete database. (Please specify your specific purpose in the "Purpose" column.)

5. After evaluation, if your application meets the authorization criteria, we will send you the cloud disk link of the complete database by email for you to download. The evaluation process generally takes less than a week.

6. The above personal information is only used for evaluation and will be kept strictly confidential.

7. Database citation formats:

① Li Z, Liang X, Liu J, et al. Dcmi: A database of chinese musical instruments[J]. 2018.

② Zhaorui Liu, & Zijin Li. (2021). Music Data Sharing Platform for Computational Musicology Research (CCMUSIC DATASET) (1.1) [Data set]. Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.5676893

! Attention: this database is free of charge. If you receive a charge by email after submitting the application form, please do not respond !
